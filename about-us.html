Our Heritage

Founded in the heart of London, Charter House Cars began with a simple vision: to create the most refined chauffeur experience in the industry. What started as a modest fleet of three luxury vehicles has grown into a premier transportation service spanning major cities across Europe.

Our founder, James Charter, established the company on principles of discretion, reliability, and impeccable service - values that continue to guide every journey we undertake today.

The Charter Standard

We don't just transport passengers - we curate experiences. Every Charter House journey is built on three pillars:

  • Uncompromising Safety: Rigorous vehicle maintenance and chauffeur training
  • Discreet Professionalism: White-glove service with absolute confidentiality
  • Tailored Comfort: Bespoke amenities for each client's preferences

This commitment has made us the preferred choice for diplomats, executives, and discerning travelers who expect nothing less than perfection.
